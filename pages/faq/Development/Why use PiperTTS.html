I was searching for a offline TTS generation that sounded somewhat realistic and was performant.


I had the following TTS options on my mind:

  • Windows System.Speech

    • Ships with Windows
    • Can't guarantee every player has the same voices installed
    • Licensing/usage terms are tied to Windows itself

  • DECtalk

    • Old school robotic voice
    • Extremely lightweight computationally
    • Licensing murky

  • Piper TTS

    • Offline
    • Produces normal sounding voices
    • Computationally lightweight, but more computationally intense then DECtalk
    • Open source (GPL-3.0)

Piper TTS ended up being the better option.
