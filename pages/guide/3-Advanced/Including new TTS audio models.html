Important

When using new voice models, make sure they are using the 'medium' quality setting.

Tip

Looking for supported voice models? Find them at huggingface.co/rhasspy/piper-voices, and preview voice samples at rhasspy.github.io/piper-samples.

You can also train your own custom voice, see the Piper training guide.

Using custom TTS voice models

You can add any Piper TTS compatible voice model to your mod.


Create a folder named exactly TTS-Company-Voices one level down in your mod's folder.

For example:

BepInEx\plugins\YourModName\TTS-Company-Voices


Place the voice model's .onnx and .onnx.json files inside that folder.

That's it.
